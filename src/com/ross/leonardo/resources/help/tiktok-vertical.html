TikTok Vertical (9:16)

What this mode does

This mode outputs a video formatted for vertical viewing (9:16), suitable for TikTok-style platforms.

It’s meant to help you quickly produce a vertical deliverable from common sources.

When to use it

  • You recorded or edited in landscape and need a vertical version.
  • You want a quick vertical export without re-editing the full project.
  • You’re preparing platform-ready clips for mobile-first viewing.

How to use it

  1. Select TikTok Vertical (9:16) mode.
  2. Choose your input file.
  3. Choose an output folder.
  4. Click Convert.
  5. Preview the output to confirm framing is acceptable before posting.

Tip: If your subject is centered, results will usually look best.

Notes

  • Vertical conversion may involve cropping or scaling depending on your source aspect ratio.
  • If important content is near the edges, you may need to reframe in an editor for best results.
  • Always preview on a phone before publishing.
